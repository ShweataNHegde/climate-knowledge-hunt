The Tech We Want Online Summit

The Tech We Want Online Summit, hosted by the Open Knowledge Foundation (OKFN), is a two day online event where experts in public interest technologies come together to discuss practical ways to create software that is helpful, easy to use, durable, and solves real-world problems.

This is a free online event open to all.

The technology professionals, developers, engineers, and programming language experts are encouraged to register and share their technology and softwares with the world.

#semanticClimate team will present their tools and software in the summit.
