Youth Interaction about climate action

#semanticClimate team will interact with middle and high school students of the underprevileged communities CBN foundation to exchange insights and expertise regarding climate knowledge. Through this interaction, participants can gain a comprehensive understanding of various aspects related to climate science, adaptation strategies, and mitigation efforts.

With this effort, we hope to empower and enrich the lives of students and lead the way for them to become climate champions!
